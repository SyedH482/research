Etiquette Guide Accessibility Needs Form

Hi! Here's an overview of some common etiquette to keep in mind in our classroom.

    Please keep chatting in the window to a minimum. Classmates who are DHH may have trouble splitting their attention between the chat window and the classroom discussion
    Please remember to turn on your captions.
    Stay muted when not speaking to not give off background noise.
    Be mindful and aware of the chat (could put reminder in chat window periodically
    Please dont speak to the interpreter, speak directly to the student.
    Overall, make this an inclusive space for everyone and be understanding of other's needs.
